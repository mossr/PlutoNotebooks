Dirichlet Distribution

The dirichlet distribution is a generalized beta distribution to multiple dimensions.

Dir⁡(θ1:n∣α1:n)=Γ(α0)∏i=1nΓ(αi)∏i=1nθiαi−1

The Dirichlet distribution has a posterior of

p(θ1:n∣α1:n,m1:n)=Dir⁡(θ1:n∣α1+m1,…,αn+mn)

and a mean vector 𝛍, whose ith component is

μi=αi∑j=1nαj.

For more notebook, see https://github.com/mossr/PlutoNotebooks
