Gibbs Sampling

Gibbs sampling is a Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) technique to indirectly sample from a joint distribution p(x) by instead drawing samples iteratively from known conditional probability distributions p(xi∣x−i) where x−i denotes all variables other than xi.
